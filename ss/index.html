Our School

Singapore School Manila is an international school that adapts the Singaporean curriculum for Preschool, Primary and Lower Secondary levels, Cambridge IGCSE for for Higher Secondary and the Cambridge International AS/A Levels or the International Baccalaureate Diploma Program (IBDP) for Pre-university.

SSM is an IB World school. As such, we are deeply committed to develop internationally-minded people who, recognising their common humanity and shared guardianship of the planet, help to create a better and more peaceful world. We are also an accredited Cambridge school.

Our Vision

We envision ourselves to be a school known for providing a high standard of quality education placing equal emphasis on all aspects of human development. Our goal is to develop global citizens who are creative, enquirers, thinkers, open-minded, knowledgable, communicators, risk-takers, principled, caring, balanced, reflective, responsible, resilient and decisive, living and promoting in others a positive outlook in life.

Our Mission

We, the SSM community, dedicate ourselves to achieve our vision by:

  • Striving for academic excellence using modern teaching strategies, disposing of the pressures and trauma associated with conventional teaching methods
  • Training our students to do higher thinking in all subjects, preparing them for a rapidly-changing world.
  • Developing the value of positivism through our educational system enabling our students to love learning and to cope well with the challenges of life.
  • Encouraging environmental and social awareness, thus empowering our students to take an active role as agents of positive change in the world.
