ABOUT MEDICARD HEALTH CHECK CARD


Set a date with Doc at MediCard free standing clinics with MediCard Health Check card. It's your first step to getting healthy!

The MediCard Health Check card is an offering from MediCard Philippines, a leading HMO provider in the country. Aimed at helping Filipinos get regular health check-ups and preventive health care services at an affordable price point, the MediCard Health Check Card is proof that MediCard has got your back.

With just a one-time payment of P500, cardholders are entitled to a one-year unlimited consultations with MediCard primary care physicians and discounts on laboratory tests. There is no age limitation and no pre-acceptance medical exam to avail of the card. Membership is good for one (1) year.

To apply for a MediCard Health Check Card, simply visit or call any MediCard free-standing clinics. For more information, go to http://www.medicardphils.com/office-clinics
