Cardio Health

"I'm too young to worry about heart disease."


The correct answer : Myth

How you live affects your risk for cardiovascular disease later in life. As early as childhood and adolescence, plague can start accumulating in the arteries and later lead to clogged arteries. Even young and middle-aged people can develop heart problems.
